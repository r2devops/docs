## 🔐 Authorization Settings

<ReleaseLabels licenses={[2]} selfManagedOnly />

You can now grant special permissions to view (read only) the analysis dashboard to user with [GitLab Auditor access right](https://docs.gitlab.com/ee/administration/auditor_users.html) or an entire group members.

![User experience demonstration of the Authorization Settings page](./authorization_settings.gif)

## 🏷️ Labels Settings

You can now create your own standards to label your templates. This feature helps you improve your control over your organization's CI/CD pipeline steps.

![User experience demonstration of the Labels Settings page](./labels_settings.gif)

## ⚙️ Minor Updates

- Improved the loading display of "Flag as Safe" action on variables.
- Update Roadmap page content
